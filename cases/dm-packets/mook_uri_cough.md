## What this case tests

The mook trap: spending less than 60 seconds in the room and discharging on the diagnosis from the door. The disambiguator is **focal pulmonary findings + (CXR if vitals warrant)**. SpO₂ 96 + HR 92 + temp 37.8 sits in the "ehh" zone where over-imaging is wasteful but under-imaging misses the camouflaged 20%. Pleuritic pain on focused history is the cheapest signal.
