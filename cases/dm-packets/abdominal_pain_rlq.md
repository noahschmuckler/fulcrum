## What this case tests

The threshold question: when does a stable-appearing reproductive-age woman with focal RLQ pain need ED transfer vs. UC workup-and-discharge? The hCG + POCUS pelvic are the decisive tools the UC has. The trap: if you don't think to draw or look, ectopic walks out — and the camouflaged ovarian cyst that doesn't need transfer looks identical from the door.
