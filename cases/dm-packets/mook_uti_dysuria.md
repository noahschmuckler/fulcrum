## What this case tests

The threshold question: when does dysuria + recurrent-UTI history get sent home with a Macrobid script vs. need a workup? The signal is the **vital-sign-plus-CVA-pain combination**. HR 96 + temp 37.5 looks fine; layering on flank pain or rigor flips the picture. Lactate is the cheap-to-order disambiguator the UC has that a primary care office doesn't. The trap is the recurrent-UTI patient who *says* "this feels like always" — and is wrong.
